AirCursor Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 29, 2026

AirCursor is a macOS utility that uses on-device camera processing to detect hand gestures for preview and calibration.

Data Collection

AirCursor does not collect, sell, rent, or share personal data. The app does not include analytics, advertising, tracking, account registration, or remote logging.

Camera Use

AirCursor requests camera access so it can detect hand gestures on your Mac. Camera frames are processed locally on the device. They are not recorded, stored, transmitted, or shared with us or any third party.

Network Use

AirCursor does not send camera frames, gesture data, or app usage data over the network.

Children's Privacy

AirCursor is not designed to collect data from anyone, including children.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new update date.

Contact

For privacy questions, use the support channel listed on the AirCursor Support page.
